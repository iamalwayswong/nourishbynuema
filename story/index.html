The nourish story

Our Mission

At Nourish by Nuema, we make bone broth the way it has always been made — brewed through the night, slowly, until what remains is only what was always meant to remain. Delivered fresh to your door.

We believe in three things:
Real ingredients. Ancient tradition. Genuine care.

We make nourishment a daily ritual. We brew with intention. We show up for our community the way good food always has.

Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Cho.

Nourishment was my first language and some languages, once learned, never really leave you. I grew up working in our family owned Chinese restaurants, the kind that stayed open every day of the year. Even through storms, power outages and holidays. In my family, nourishment lived in the action. In the food. In the door that stayed open for anyone who needed it. That is how we show up — not with words but with action and what we put on the table.

After years of traveling and exploring different careers, New York City called me back. And slowly, I found myself back in the kitchen — this time making broth.

Simple ingredients. A long and patient process. The kind of care that can't be rushed.

First for myself. Then for my best friend when she became pregnant, I brewed the bone broth instinctively with the ancient Chinese ingredients I had always known. That was the moment Nourish by Nuema began.

New York City — I'd love to share this language with you.
